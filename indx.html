our services

London House Café to suit all tastes; From classic coffee drinks like cappuccino and espresso, to innovative options with distinctive flavours. It also offers a selection of delicious desserts, including fresh cakes and tarts. The place is ideal for meeting friends, working sessions, or even for enjoying a book in a quiet atmosphere. If you are looking for a moment that combines luxury and comfort, London House Café is the perfect destination.

Classic London latte

The perfect blend of rich espresso and skillfully brewed milk, garnished with a touch of coffee art on top. Offers a smooth, warm taste that embodies British elegance.

English caramel cake

Irresistible dessert! Fresh dates with rich caramel sauce provide a sweet experience with a traditional touch that reflects classic English cuisine.

Dark chocolate mocha

A drink that combines the strength of espresso with the warmth of premium dark chocolate, topped with whipped cream for the perfect finishing touch.
